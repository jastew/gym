What is the difference between you guys and "Globo-muscle gym" ?

 

We understand how intimidating joining a "normal" gym can be, so we have created an environment for people to come to leave the stress of everyday life, and take an hour just for themselves. Here, you can surround yourself with a community of positive, friendly people, Where each person cheers on the next and helps one another to reach there goal.
