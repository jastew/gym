ABOUT US

Primal Fitness is a semi-private gym that specialises in Personal Training and Small Group Training. The gym is headquartered in Greenhills industrial estate , Drogheda and was founded in 2014 on the principles of Community, Coaching and Results .Dont just take our word for it, check out some of our transformation photos.

 

Our members come from all walks of life, ages, shapes, sizes and everything in between. No two of our clients are the same so we don't treat them that way, It is a combination of this philosophy along with our passion to get people happier and healthier that sets us apart from the rest .

 

We welcome you to stop by, check us out, and hopefully work with us to achieve your goals .
